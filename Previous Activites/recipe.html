Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C
  2. Mix all dry ingredients together
  3. Add eggs and milk, mix until smooth

  5. Bake for 30 minutes
